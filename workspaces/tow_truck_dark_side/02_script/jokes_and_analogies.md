# Jokes And Analogies

## Safe analogy options

- A growing tow bill can work like a meter that keeps running after the ride already ended.
- Once the car is in the lot, the negotiation can feel less like shopping and more like paying a release fee on your own property.
- A vague quote in a high-pressure business can act like bait. The real decision arrives after the customer has fewer options.
